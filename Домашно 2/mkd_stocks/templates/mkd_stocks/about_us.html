About us

MKD StockSpot is an advanced and innovative digital tool, specially developed for the Macedonian financial market. Our platform provides centralized and efficient tracking of stocks and financial performance of companies listed on the Macedonian Stock Exchange. With a focus on accuracy and timeliness, MKD StockSpot offers investors and financial analysts reliable and easy access to up-to-date and detailed data on stock movements and the performance of Macedonian companies.

Our mission

Our mission is to enhance the transparency of the Macedonian financial market by providing accurate, up-to-date, and analytical financial data, enabling investors and analysts to make informed and rational decisions. The platform aims to become a reliable source of financial information for all users, regardless of their experience. We offer advanced analytical insights and indicators that help in understanding market trends and making smart investment decisions.
